Package Items

Below you can find the several package items. Some of these items can also be purchased individually. For more info about them, please contact the committee member assigned to your company.

Curricula

Every company that acquires a sponsor package will be given a link to download student's CVs, gathered during the event and after.

Stand

You'll be granted a stand inside our venue where you'll be able to contact with all our attendees.

Presentation

The presentation allows the company to introduce and explain to the students their field of work, the projects the company has been working on, the everyday life inside the office and also the job opportunities that might be available to the students in the company. The goal is to sell the company to students.

20 min session divided as such:
10 mins for company pitch
10 mins of networking with breakfast

Rooms with capacity for: 50

Workshop

The workshops usually occur in the late afternoon. The theme is a choice of the company, approved by the organizing committee. They also have a limited attendance which means they have to be booked by the students before the event starts.

Duration: 1h30min
Rooms with capacity for: 50

Keynote Sponsorship

The company can have publicity materials at the entry of the auditorium at the beginning, during, and at the end of the sponsored keynote. This can include the company's roll-up, merchandising or other items to be agreeded with us.

Speed Dates

A speed date will consist in a 15 minutes job interview with a student, previously booked, in a room inside the venue. Each company will have a limited number of speed dates. For more information, please contact us.

15 mins booking:
10 mins interview
5 mins tolerance
